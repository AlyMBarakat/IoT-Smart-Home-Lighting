Smart Home Lighting

IOT Smart Home Lighting

This website allows you to toggle lighting in diffrent rooms of your house, the light bulbs are wired to NodeMCU:ESP32 that is connected to the Internet through the house WiFi. MQTT protocol is used for communication.

This website connects to MQTT Broker using MQTT over WebSockets.

The Broker is hosted on DigitalOcean.
